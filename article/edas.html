EDAS Account Registration Tutorial

1. Go to the website https://edas.info/N24559

If you are already have an EDAS account, please login to submit your paper using your registered account.

2. If you don't have an EDAS account, create a new one

One must include the following information (RED indicates compulsory):
  • Title of the person
  • First name, Middle initial and Last name
  • Affiliation
  • Status (student, faculty, post-doc, industry, government, accompanying person)
  • Job Title (professor, director, manager etc.)
  • Mailing Address
  • Street Address (PO Box, if street address is not available)
  • City
  • If US, Name of the State; if Canada, Name of Province
  • Postal Code (Zip Code)
  • Country
  • E-mail Address
  • Phone and Fax

Enter your details and click Submit to create your account.

3. Your account has been created and you will receive e-mail containing login “e-mail address” and ‘password’

4. Once your EDAS account has been created, you will be able to use this account to register your paper and submit your full paper. This section will guide you through your paper registration

  • Please ensure that you are using the correct account to submit your manuscript, as an invoice will be generated for this account.

5. Sign in to your account.

6. Once you are logged in, click the symposium that matches on your paper to be registered.

7. Enter your paper details and click Submit to register your paper.


8. Your paper has successfully been registered. You will now be able to view your paper in your EDAS account.

If you want to add authors, click “add author” and input EDAS’s email. Now you can submit your paper by clicking “upload the manuscript”.

If you still require further assistance, please feel free to contact us.


EDAS Full Paper Submission Tutorial

1. Sign in to your account.

2. Go to your EDAS profile for ICST Conference and click “blue button” in Review manuscript to upload your full paper.

3. By clicking upload button, you will direct to submission page. New window will appear, through which you can upload your full paper. Attached your full paper, and click Submit.

  • Notes :
  • Pay attention to the paper format allowed in ICST Conference
  • You can upload the following file types: PDF, Microsoft Word (doc but not docx), Micrsoft Open Office XML format (Word 2007,docx).

4. Your full paper has been submitted.

If you require further assistance with submitting your full paper, please email the conference’s contact person.
